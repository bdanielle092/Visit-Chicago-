Chicago City

Chicago is the most populous city in the state of Illinois in the United States. It is the seat of Cook County, the second
most populous county in the United States after Los Angeles County, California. It owns less than 1% of its territory
in DuPage County. It was founded in 1833 near a berth between the Great Lakes and the Mississippi River basin.

With more than 2.7 million inhabitants, according to the 2020 census it is the most populous city in the Midwest region
and the third most populous in the United States, after New York and Los Angeles. It is the fifth most densely populated
locality in Illinois. Its metropolitan area, commonly known as "Greater Chicago", is the 27th most populous urban
agglomeration in the world, home to more than 9.6 million people spread across the US states of Illinois, Indiana and
Wisconsin. Just over 21% of the total population of Illinois lives in Chicago.
